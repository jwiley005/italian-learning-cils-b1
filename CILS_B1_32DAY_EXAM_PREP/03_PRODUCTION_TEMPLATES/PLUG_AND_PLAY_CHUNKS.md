## üìö HOW TO USE THESE CHUNKS

### Strategy 1: Mix and Match
- Choose chunks from different sections to build your response
- Don't use the same chunks every time (variety shows fluency)
- Example: Opinion starter + First reason + Example + Conclusion

### Strategy 2: Personalize
- Add your personal details to make chunks authentic
- Replace [blanks] with specific information
- Example: "Nel tempo libero mi piace camminare per Roma"

### Strategy 3: Practice Combinations
- Week 1: Practice 3-4 chunks per day

- Week 3: Use chunks naturally without thinking
