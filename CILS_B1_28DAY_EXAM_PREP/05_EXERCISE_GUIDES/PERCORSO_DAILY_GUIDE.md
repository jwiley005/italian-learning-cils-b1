## üìñ HOW TO USE EACH UNIT (35 min Daily)

### **Listening Practice** (15 min)

**Strategy** (from Percorso p.17):
1. **1st Listen** (no reading!): Just listen, get global meaning
2. **2-min Pause**: Read questions quickly
3. **2nd Listen**: Match audio to answers
4. **2-min Check**: Verify answers
5. **3 min**: Copy to answer sheet (practice for exam!)

**Where to Find Audio**:
- CD included with book (rip to phone/computer)
- Or download from Guerra Edizioni website
- Or use OCR scan + text-to-speech (not ideal but works)

**Important**: Percorso audio = EXACT exam format (speed, accent, structure)

---

### **Reading + Grammar** (20 min)

**Reading Section** (10 min):
- Read entire text FIRST (2 min) - get context
- Answer Vero/Falso questions (6 min)
- Check answer key (2 min)
- **Common Trap**: Info is paraphrased, not word-for-word!

**Grammar Cloze** (10 min):
- Read entire text with gaps (2 min)
- Fill 6 gaps (6 min) - all 3 options = same grammar category!
- Check answer key (2 min)
- **Use your Grammar Cheat Sheet** if stuck!

**Focus**: Don't just get right answer - understand WHY wrong answers are wrong!
