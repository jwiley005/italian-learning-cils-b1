| Person | Singular | Plural | Example |
|--------|----------|--------|---------|
| 1st | mi (me) | ci (us) | Maria **mi** vede. |

### **Double Object Pronouns** (Indirect + Direct)

Indirect changes form when combined with direct:
- mi ‚Üí **me**
- ti ‚Üí **te**
- gli/le ‚Üí **glie-** (attached!)
- ci ‚Üí **ce**
- vi ‚Üí **ve**

Examples:
- Me **lo** dai? = Will you give it to me? (me + lo = me lo)
- **Glie**lo porto = I bring it to him/her (**glie** + lo = glielo)
- **Ce**ne danno = They give us some (ce + ne = ce ne)

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ PARTICLES CI AND NE (15% of Cloze Test)

### **CI = "There" (Locative)**

Replaces a place name:
- Vai a Roma? ‚Üí S√¨, **ci** vado domani. (ci = a Roma)
- Sei mai stato in Sicilia? ‚Üí No, non **ci** sono mai stato. (ci = in Sicilia)

**Position**: BEFORE the verb

### **NE = "Of it/them" (Partitive)**

Replaces quantities or things introduced by "di":
- Vuoi del caff√®? ‚Üí S√¨, **ne** prendo un po'. (ne = di caff√®)
- Quanti libri hai? ‚Üí **Ne** ho tre. (ne = di libri)
- Parliamo di politica? ‚Üí S√¨, **ne** parliamo spesso. (ne = di politica)

**Past Participle Agreement with NE**:
- Quante mele hai comprato? ‚Üí **Ne** ho comprat**e** tre. (agrees with "mele" = feminine plural)

### **CI + NE Together = CE NE**

- Quante sedie ci sono? ‚Üí **Ce ne** sono dieci. (ce = ci, ne = di sedie)
- CI changes to CE before NE!

**Verb Agreement Trap**:

- "Il pianeta che ci ________" (circonda / circondano / circondiamo?)

## üéØ EXAM STRATEGY FOR CLOZE TEST

### **Step-by-Step (15 minutes total)**

1. **Read ENTIRE text first** (1 min) ‚Üí Get context
2. **For EACH gap** (2 min per gap √ó 6 gaps = 12 min):
   - Read full sentence (don't stop at gap!)
   - Identify grammatical category (all 3 options = same type!)
   - Eliminate based on AGREEMENT (gender, number, person)
   - Check context/meaning if 2 options remain
3. **Re-read with your answers** (2 min) ‚Üí Does it flow?

### **Quick Elimination Tricks**

**If options are pronouns**:
- Check gender/number of noun they replace
- Example: "Maria? **La** vedo domani" (NOT "lo" - Maria = feminine!)

**If options are prepositions**:
- Check if it's a collocation (verb + prep combo)
- Example: "Parlo **con** Maria" (NOT "a" - parlare con = talk with)

**If options are indefinites**:
- Check if noun is singular/plural
- Example: "**Ogni** giorno" (NOT "tutti" - ogni = singular only!)

**If options are verbs**:
- Find the subject (might be in relative clause!)
- Check person/number agreement
