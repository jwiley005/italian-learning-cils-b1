### The CILS B1 Cittadinanza Exam

**Format**: 4 sections, all must be passed (non-capitalizable)
**Duration**: 2 hours 15 minutes total
**Passing Score**: 50% minimum per section (7/14 points)

| Section | Duration | Format | Points | Pass |
|---------|----------|--------|--------|------|
| **A. Ascolto (Listening)** | 20 min | 2 listening tasks: 6 short dialogues + 12 Vero/Falso | 14 | 7 |
| **B. Lettura + Grammatica (Reading + Grammar)** | 40 min | 12 Vero/Falso reading comprehension + 6 cloze grammar gaps | 14 | 7 |
| **C. Produzione Scritta (Writing)** | 25 min | Write 80-120 word email (formal register) | 14 | 7 |
| **D. Produzione Orale (Speaking)** | 15 min | Speak on 2-3 topics without preparation (150-200 words total) | 14 | 7 |

**Critical**: Must score ‚â•7 points on ALL 4 sections. If you score 13/14 on listening but 4/14 on grammar, you **fail the entire exam** and must retake all 4 sections.
